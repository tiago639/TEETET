ABOUT

A new vision for your legal needs.

Our firm began its activities in 2008 with the vision of entrepreneurship in law, establishing innovative parameters in the legal segment, inspired by the values of its founder and patron Antônio Krepp Filho, who served as a Judge for 34 years in the region.


In just a few years, we have distinguished ourselves for our effectiveness in audits and consultancies to companies in various sectors, including multinationals and public administration.


We have a team of professionals attentive to jurisdictional developments in Brazil and worldwide, as well as a highly qualified body of lawyers. Values such as ethics, professionalism, proximity, commitment and objectivity are differentials we establish from the first consultation, with the purpose of safeguarding our clients' rights effectively and proactively.

MISSION

To deliver innovative legal solutions with agility and quality, combining technology and personalized service, contributing to the achievement of excellence for our clients throughout the national territory.

VISION

To be recognized nationally as a leader in full service law, through strategic and effective tools that promote the development of our clients and the satisfaction of our employees, in a humanized and sustainable environment.

VALUES

MCK provides personalized service of quality and innovation, guided by the relationship of trust, ethics and loyalty of the team with clients and partners, through performance sustained by technology and efficiency that generates results.
